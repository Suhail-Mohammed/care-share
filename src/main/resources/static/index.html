How It Works

1. Register & Create Profile
Sign up with your email and set your location to see nearby listings.
Add your photo, name, interests and preferences for smoother connections.
2. List Your Items
Upload photos, description, condition, and price or exchange preference.
Categorize your item for better visibility to the right audience.
3. Browse Listings
Search for items by category, location, or condition.
Use filters to narrow down to what you really want.
4. Chat Securely
